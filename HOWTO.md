## Requirements

* Debian Wheezy/Ubuntu 12.04+
* Windows 7 and MINGW

## Commands

* Movement: Arrow Keys or IJKL
* Inventory: Escape
* Grab: g
* Drop: d (From Inventory)
* Use: u (From Inventory)
* Equip: e (From Inventory)
* Wield: w (From Inventory)
* Quit: Close Window
* Help Screen: ?
